Complex
=======

`WMA link <https://reference.wolfram.com/language/ref/Complex.html>`_


:code:`Complex`
    is the head of complex numbers.

:code:`Complex` [:math:`a`, :math:`b`]
    constructs the complex number :code:`:math:`a` + I :math:`b`` .





>>> Head[2 + 3*I]

    =
:math:`\text{Complex}`


>>> Complex[1, 2/3]

    =
:math:`1+\frac{2 I}{3}`


>>> Abs[Complex[3, 4]]

    =
:math:`5`
